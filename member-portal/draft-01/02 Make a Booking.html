Terms & Conditions
Booking Terms & Conditions

Each can only book a shooting session of maximum 4 hrs per day. Slots are available for booking 1 week in advance. Please note that time slots are fixed, hence do arrive on time to enjoy your full session. E.g. if you made a booking between 1pm – 3pm slot, you can choose to arrive anytime in between 1pm to 3pm, but the end time is still fixed at 3pm as per your time slot. Walk-ins are subjected to availability of slots. You are required to manage your bookings and cancellations on your own.

Cancellation Policies

The range fee will be charged for no shows and late cancellations less than 12 hours before the session. For Unlimited package holders, your booking privileges (as well as your pre-existing bookings) will be revoked for 1 week. You will still be able to walk in if the range are not full.
